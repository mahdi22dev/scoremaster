How to use this app:

Enter the expected goals (XG) for both teams and click 'Submit Prediction' to calculate the likely score outcomes for the match.

You can get XG values from sports apps and websites such as Flashscore, which provide detailed match statistics. Please note that XG data is generally available only for matches in major leagues, such as the English Premier League, La Liga in Spain, and other top-tier competitions.
